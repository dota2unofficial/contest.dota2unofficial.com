GRAND PRIZE

Dota 2 Unofficial wants to give back to the community that allows it to exist, while also bringing some fun to these trying times. So dust off your keyboards, and clear those cobwebs off your brain, it's time to make this quarantine exciting again!

The Arcade Festival invites any and all creators to make new custom games for the Dota 2 Arcade. Bring some smiles to everyone stuck in their homes, and be rewarded for your efforts!

$1,000 USD

Most Original Game Award

For daring to be new and fresh and succeeding in doing so

$1,000 USD

The People's Choice Award

For the game which captures the most players' hearts

$1,000 USD

The Dev's Choice Award

For the game most loved by the creator's own peers

$1,000 USD

Technical Achievement Award

For the game that pushes the boundaries of what Dota 2 modding is capable of

Just as Winter's cold darkness always gives way to the warm light of Spring, so will our current predicament eventually end. And just as nature always regrows and renews itself, humanity will do the same.

Your entry must be heavily themed around Regrowth in some way. It can be a central gameplay element, the game's setting, or something else. The relationship can be direct - as in, plant death and regrowth - or much more abstract, but it must be central to your game's idea.

THEME: REGROWTH

• A panel of judges from Dota 2 Unofficial will define the 'best game' winner, based on simple majority.
• In case of a tie, the judges must debate among themselves and unanimously select one of the tied entries as the winner.
• All members of Dota 2 Unofficial, as well as any creators involved in the judging panel, are forbidden from entering the tournament themselves.

RULES

On Judging

• Each runner-up award will be granted to the game which fulfills its criteria. Runner-up awards cannot be granted to the winner of the 'best game' award.
• Most original game award: chosen by the judging panel, with the same rules as the 'best game' award.
• Technical achievement award: chosen by the judging panel, with the same rules as the 'best game' award.
• The people's choice award: awarded to the game with the highest average player count for a period after the submission deadline.
• The dev's choice award: chosen by simple majority vote among all veteran modders of the moddota.com community. In case of a tie, it will be broken by the judging panel. We will manually monitor votes to make sure there is no fraudulent manipulation with duped accounts and the like.

On runner-up Awards' Criteria

• Every entry must be a custom game heavily based around the "Regrowth" theme.
• Custom games that have started development before this announcement cannot be submitted.
• If a custom game is not heavily on theme or it is strongly suspected of being in development before this announcement, it may be disqualified at the judging panel's discretion.
• Entries must be designed to be multiplayer, with a minimum of 4 players.
• Entries must be submitted before 00:00 GMT of xxxxx xxst, 2020.
• Entries must include the following text in their description, so that they can be found easily by the judging panel: "Candidate for 2020 custom game context - contest.dota2unofficial.com".

On runner-up Awards' Criteria

Rules may change as the contest progresses, we will try to avoid this but there might be unforeseen reasons why we need to update these rules.
