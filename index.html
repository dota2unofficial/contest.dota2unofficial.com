A Dota 2 Custom Game Contest

Dota 2 Unofficial launches its first contest for Dota Custom Games developers and offers a total prize pool of $14,000 USD for the winning Custom Games.

Best Game - Grand Prize

For the overall best custom game of the festival

Just as Winter's cold darkness always gives way to the warm light of Spring, so will our current predicament eventually end. And just as nature always regrows and renews itself, humanity will do the same.

Your entry must be heavily themed around Regrowth in some way. It can be a central gameplay element, the game's setting, or something else. The relationship can be direct - as in, plant death and regrowth - or much more abstract, but it must be central to your game's idea.

THEME: REGROWTH

• Every entry must be a custom game heavily based around the "Regrowth" theme.
• Custom games that have started development before this announcement cannot be submitted.
• If a custom game is not heavily on theme or it is strongly suspected of being in development before this announcement, it may be disqualified at the judging panel's discretion.
• Entries must be designed to be multiplayer, with a minimum of 4 players.
• Entries must be submitted before 00:00 GMT of 1st of October, 2020.
• Entries must include the following text in their workshop description, so that they can be easily found by the judging panel: "Candidate for unofficial custom game contest - contest.dota2unofficial.com".

RULES

Requirements for Entry

• A panel of judges from Dota 2 Unofficial will define the 'best game' winner, based on simple majority.

On judging the best game of the contest

• Each category award will be granted to the game which fulfills its criteria. Category awards cannot be granted to the winner of the 'best game' award.
• 'Technical Achievement' and 'Most Original' winners will be decided by a panel of judges from Dota 2 Unofficial.
• The people's choice award will be chosen by which custom game has the most active players on average. After the deadline for submission has ended, Dota 2 Unofficial will track the average number of players for each competing custom game, the custom game that has the highest average player count (up until the end of the judging process) will be the winner of this category.
• The dev's choice award will be by simple majority vote among all veteran modders of the moddota.com community. In case of a tie, it will be broken by the judging panel. Dota 2 Unofficial will manually monitor votes to make sure there is no fraudulent manipulation with duped accounts and the like. If any other developer outside of ModDota would like to be part of this voting panel, they can contact contest coordinators on the ModDota Discord channel, if they can prove they have created a significant custom game they can be part of this developer vote.

On judging the category winners

Rules may change as the contest progresses, we will try to avoid this but there might be unforeseen reasons why we need to update these rules.

FAQ

When is the due date for submission?
End of September GMT Time, countdown to end of contest.
Where is a good place to get started with Dota 2 modding?
This is a good resource to get started. Also if you have questions, you should come to the help desk discord channel to get help here.
Can we work in groups?
Yes.
Can we publish before the due date?
Yes, you can publish as soon as you like. We recommend that you upload at least a week or a few days before the due date so that you can get public feedback on your game, to discover bugs/improvements, grow a player base and to maximise your chance of winning.
Can I use code or assets from previous custom games?
To an extent yes. You can use utility functions and general framework/libraries from previous custom games, nearly all custom games use libraries in one way or another. But if a significant portion of your custom game is code from previous custom games, this may get you disqualified, this is at the discretion of the judging panel, if you need further clarification, don't hesitate to communicate with contest coordinators on the discord channel here.

Furthermore you should only use assets you have a legal right to use, avoid using copyrighted assets.
Help! I have more questions that are not answered here
Feel free to come ask any questions on the contest discord channel on the moddota discord server.
